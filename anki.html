Creating a practical Flashcard Design

Now Anki is a very handy flashcard SRS (Spaced Repetition System) application that is, in my opinion, absolutely fantastic for optimizing memory retention on any subject you wish you use it for.

Sadly the default look and style you get out the box is practically non-existant and that doesn't help to boost motivation levels when you sit down to go through your deck. Thankfully we are given the power of HTML & CSS to customize it to our needs and that's exactly what I did.
